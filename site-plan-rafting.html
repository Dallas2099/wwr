Overview

Purpose

Our website is designed to showcase thrilling whitewater rafting adventures, promote safe outdoor recreation and increase bookings for our guided river tours in Colorado. Through engaging content, scenic visuals, and easy navigation, we aim to attract adventure seekers and help them plan their ideal rafting experience.

Audience

Our target audience includes outdoor enthusiasts, families and tourists aged 20â€“50 with a moderate to high income who are looking for a fun and safe adventure. Many of them are first-time rafters or vacationers visiting Colorado. They want an exciting and memorable activity that is easy to book and well-reviewed. Most users will access our site using mobile devices while traveling, though some may use laptops or desktops while planning from home.
